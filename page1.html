How to make Bubbles

I believe that many of you here have at least played before with bubbles in your childhood.

And what's better to relieve your childhood memories by making bubbles by yourself!

Here in this website shows some ways that you can make bubbles solution for you, or your friends or child

Have fun!

Video by ENMVJ in Scratch

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups of Water
  • 1/2 Cups Dish Soap
  • 1 Cups of Sugar
  • 1 Bubble Wand
  • 1 Small Bowl

Steps:

  1. Add the water into a small bowl.
  2. Pour in the dish soap.
  3. Add sugar into the mixture and stir gently until the sugar is dissolved.
  4. Woohoo! The bubble solution is done!
