Some best ideas to Play with Bubbles

October 30, 2021 by Teλi

Did you get bored during the holidays and have no idea on what to do? Have no fear! For this webpage shall provide you with some activities that you may not have done it after your childhood! And what better way to relieve childhood memories while playing with something from your childhood than Bubbles!

|1| Painting with Bubbles

Making use of food dyes or paints, allowing your bubbles fly with flying colors!

Catch those colored bubbles on a piece of white construction paper, and there you go! The colored bubbles will pop on your paper and beautiful artwork is produced!

|2| Layered Bubbles

Ever heard of kue lapis, the multi layer cake? Why don't we try something similar to that, but with the theme of bubbles?

This activity requires you to try to make one huge bubble, and smaller ones inside of the bubble! Also, if you are feeling awesome, you can push yourself further by making more bubbles inside each bubbles without popping them! .+.

Video by ENMVJ in Scratch

|3| DodgeBubbles

This time, what better way to modify the game of DodgeBall to fit to our theme of Bubbles?

Play with a group of friends of family members, with each person blowing bubbles! But beware, if you get backfired cause you blew too much bubbles, that those bubbles touch you, you are OUT! Fun isn't it?

|4| Unordinary Bubble Wand

How are you going to play with bubble without a wand? Unleash your creavity

You can go with common solutions, strings. Or something different, a paper cone. Hmmmmm.... Is there any other better solution you have? If you want, feel free to post your experience on your social media! Perhaps I may be able to find your post, then marvel at your masterpiece! :]

|5| Homemade Gifts

What better way to say thanks to someone then giving self-made gifts?

Firstly, you need to prepare a conatiner [best if it is a transparent, small one]

Next, you can prepare the bubble solutions first hand, giving it some dye or paint if you wish to, and fill it in the conatiner!

Lastly, what the use of a bubble solution without a bubble wand to blow them out? Making use of wires, you can bend then into shapes, like hearts or stars, and hook it somewhere on your container!

There, your homemade gifts are done! Gift them to anyone whom you want to, or you can give them to me! Hehe. .^.
